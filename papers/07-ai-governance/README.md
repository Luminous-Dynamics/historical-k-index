## Governance Design Informed by K-Index

### Principles
- Match governance mechanisms to current K(t) level
- Don't design for K(t) we don't have
- Build K(t) alongside AI capability
- Prioritize H‚ÇÉ investment

### Institutional Design Options
- **Low K(t) path**: National regulation, bilateral agreements
- **Medium K(t) path**: Regional blocs, voluntary standards
- **High K(t) path**: International treaty, global governance body

### Recommended Trajectory
- Current: Low K(t) mechanisms (realistic)
- Build: Trust infrastructure for AI cooperation
- Target: Medium K(t) mechanisms by 2030
- Aspire: High K(t) mechanisms by 2040
