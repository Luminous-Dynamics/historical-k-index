1. Open any HTML document in your browser
2. Click the **"üñ®Ô∏è Print / Save as PDF"** button (top right corner)
3. Browser will open print dialog
4. Select **"Save as PDF"** as the destination/printer
5. Ensure settings:

7. Choose location and filename
